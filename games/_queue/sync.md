## Game Concept

Players navigate an abstract minimalist isometric space, moving themselves and objects with heavy physics. Work together to reach synchronized goals while manipulating the environment with push and freeze mechanics.

## Core Mechanic

Weight-based cooperative movement, object manipulation, and player synergy (boost/carry). Environmental puzzles require careful coordination to solve.

## Visual Style

Retro pixel art. Abstract geometric shapes and minimalist palette. Isometric camera angle with clear depth perception.

## Win Condition

All players must reach the goal location at the same time. Success is synchronized arrival, not individual completion.

## Pacing

Short, focused puzzle sessions. Each puzzle is self-contained for quick play.

## Tone

Chill and relaxing. Stress-free cooperation without fail states or harsh penalties.

## Notes

- Heavy physics creates natural challenge and requires deliberate, coordinated movement
- Players can carry/boost each other for synergy — these are core to puzzle solving
- Environmental puzzles use push (move objects) and freeze (halt movement/time) mechanics
- Gracefully support 1–4 players (solo, pairs, full squad all viable)
- Synchronized win condition adds cooperative tension without failure/restart loops
- Isometric view allows depth clarity for physics-based interaction
- Quick sessions mean no grinding; players can play, win, replay

## Build Constraints

- Python + pygame
- No external assets required
- Must launch cleanly and exit cleanly on ATTACK+JUMP
